Digital Designs

Brand Logo

This was a group project where we each create our version of a logo, with inverse colours, black and white, and original. My version of this brand was an idea from the name, since Super Nova means "death of stars". It also means "Birth of new stars". Therefore, the colourscheme I chose is orange and blue, because newers stars are blue while older stars are orange. The loop in the middle shows the transition between newer and older stars, since it is a cycle.

Application used: Adobe Illustrator

Castle on Ice

We learnt of many ways to put text on images, and how to mask and edit photos in Photoshop. Using the provided images, I masked the castle and placed it in an icy background. To make it seem more realistic, I copied and pasted the castle into the water and edited the hue to make it seem more like a reflection. After experimenting with different fonts and colours to see which would complement the image best, I added the text onto the image This is the end result of the Castle on Ice.

Application used: Adobe Photoshop
