Exercise Tracker

Our task was to create an Exercise tracker using plugins we learnt in class. The logo was made using illustrator with blocks to shape a dumbbell, while also having ET in the center, which stands for Exercise Tracker. The colourscheme used is pastel/light, which is easier to look at.

First section: User can enter their username, and when they click on "Let's Go!", they are directed to the second section. User can click on the "What is Exercise Tracker?" to toggle the definition.

Second section: The entered username on the first section will now be displayed at the top right, and hovering over shows a tippy to click if user wishes to change username. Clicking it will bring the user back to the first section. Below that are the levels the user can click to complete and unlock the other levels.

Third section: When the user clicks on the level, they need to enter the number of km, and when all 7 days are filled, they can click on "create data" and a chart will be created on the right. If the requirements are met, "Next level" button will be unlocked and clicking it brings the user back to the second section to complete the next challenge.
