Sketches

Hand Sketch

Anatomy is really important, and it is also not easily perfected. This was a practise sketch for hands while also trying different brushes and colour schemes.

I tried going for a paler hand since it was bloody, and was holding onto a prickly rose. I used hard pen brush for outlines, rose brush for the rose itself, and bleed watercolour for the splatter. Overall, this was an experiment that I was satisfied with.

Application used: ibisPaint X

Original Character commission

My friend, whom is an aspiring writer, gave me descriptions for her original character in the story and art style from My Hero Academia. I tried to replicate her idea as my own definitions, and used the main character of the actual series as a reference.

Colourscheme and design are from my friend's description.

Application used: ibisPaint X

まふまふ (mafumafu) Fanart

As a fan of a Japanese cover artist mafumafu, I wanted to draw his visual character to practise and also respect what he does and the effort he puts in to entertain us. The colourscheme was mostly his original character's, but I tweaked it and adjusted to my own style, with whiteish blue hair and shirt and red eyes. The shadows are used with an airbrush to enhance dimensions.

Application used: Traditional sketching, outline and colours in ibisPaint X

MapleStorySEA Character Style

My character from the game MapleStorySEA was used as reference when designing this. I tried experementing with a different style, using colours with little outline. I also used different brushes (oil brush for hair) to get different depths when colouring.

Colourscheme and design was improvised based on my own character in the game.

Application used: Traditional sketching, outline and colours in ibisPaint X
