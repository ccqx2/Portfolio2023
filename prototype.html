Sports E-Commerce Website Prototype

There are many e-commerce websites in the world. This is one of the e-commerce website prototypes I have done. The theme was a sports website, and I thought it would be nice if there were images in the background such that the site does not look plain.

The logo is made with Adobe Illustrator, which shoes the brand name and the logo which is made of sports clothes to give an idea of what this brand is about. The colourscheme is blue and red to show vibrance.

Kinesthetic App Prototype

There are many people with disabilities, so I have made an app prototype to help a group of them. This application is based on the company ElderHelp by TOUCH Community Service. You are able to press on buttons for the prototype, however the enlargement is not included.

I thought it would be nice for the theme to be vintage since it is an app for the elderly. I also thought of including many pictures so that it is not so wordy, and to make the app more pleasing to look at. Similar to the Sports Website Prototype, the logo is made with Adobe Illustrator. I designed glasses above the brand name to emphasise that the application is meant to help elderlies, especially those who have difficulty seeing.
